Hafa Adai!

It is our honor to invite you to become a member of the Guam Women's Chamber of Commerce. We are a diverse group of women advocates who share the vision of Guam's economy being driven and influenced by the equal participation of women in business. We look forward to having your voice and participation toward this effort and also offering you and your business a valuable slate of Membership Benefits.

Member Benefits

Joining your local Chamber gives you access to a range of exclusive member benefits, designed to contribute towards the success of your business.

Non Profit Membership

Non profit membership is for business that are not for profit and are not self-employed

No fee

Bronze Membership

Bronze membership is for business with less than 50 employees that are for profit. Benefits include:

  • 15% discount on events
  • 10% discount on advertising

$50 / month

Silver Membership

Silver membership is for businesses with less than 250 employees that are for profit. Additional benefits include:

  • 50% discount on events
  • 50% discount on advertising
  • Free participation in the trade fair

$100 / month

Gold Membership

Gold membership is for businesses with more than 250 employees that are for profit. Additional benefits include:

  • 50% discount on events
  • 50% discount on advertising
  • Free participation in the trade fair
  • 5 featured ads per year
  • 1 free training per quarter

$250 / month
